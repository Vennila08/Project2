The Holy Cross Girl's Higher Secondary School

Holy Cross Girls Higher Secondary School, located in Batlagundu, Tamil Nadu, is a renowned institution managed by the Sisters of the Holy Cross . Dedicated to providing quality education, the school focuses on the holistic development of its students by blending academics with moral and spiritual values. Catering exclusively to girls, it offers classes from primary to higher secondary levels under the Tamil Nadu State Board curriculum. Known for its disciplined environment, dedicated faculty, and strong emphasis on extracurricular activities, the school nurtures leadership and social responsibility in its students, fostering a well-rounded education.

General Informations

  • Location: Batlagundu, a town in the Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu, known as a gateway to Kodaikanal.
  • Management: The school is managed by the Sisters of the Holy Cross, a Catholic religious congregation dedicated to education and social service.
  • Type: Girls-only school, providing education from primary to higher secondary levels.
